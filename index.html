You should LISTEN

Another British band and oddly another success, at least for me. From Alternative Rock to Electronica and from there to Hip Hop music, they perform excellent music. You should definitely give it a go.

With their free life and music style, love against their hometown RHCP is a classic if you ask me. Recently i'm into funk music and this band actually rocking that field. I've known that band for a long time but i haven't notice how good they were. I have to give the credit and suggest my favorite song of them.

Maybe not a known band for many people but sometimes these bands perform beautiful musics and yes this is one of them. If you give it a go you should be able to hear great rythm, it actually gives you that smooth feeling. All beyond that mostly they managed to draw my attention with their music video. If you interested you can click here to watch.

IDFC. To be honest just the name explains a lot but for some personal facts this song covers up different and huge space in my heart.
