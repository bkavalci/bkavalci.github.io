CHEF

One of those inspiration movies. While I was watching, at the every minute of movie I expected something horrible to happen but at the end of it, that didn't happen and that's one the things that I find charming about these kind of movies. I'm sick of that cliche and after a long time, i'm glad that I finally found a movie, literally good for my taste (I hate myself for that joke). But i'm a person who enjoy cooking and that movie made me go out, get some Corona Cerveza, cook for myself a brilliant dinner at the middle of a night. In the other hand, the growing relationship between Carl Casper (chef) and Percy allowing us to witness beautiful dad-son time. Well like I always say and if this article is here obviously I suggest you to watch this but what if you into cooking too?..

AMERICAN HORROR STORY

Horror movies? I always drop a question mark after them cos they don't do their main purpose, "terrify me". To be honest I can't say that "I was too scared" at this one as well, at least it didn't give me the feeling of Outlast or Amnesia and TWD when I was 12. But there is something else with this show, yes it is dark and yes it is not for everyone and because of that something else is different and that is plural kind of horror. American Horror Story offers us that. I haven't finish all the seasons yet but even right now I consider this show as good enough for "suggestable category", especially with the actoring skills of Evan Peters this show worth to watch.

LUCIFER

Unfortunately Lucifer, who had really successfull 3 seasons is about to remove from our screens. Even if they remove it and cut the show at half I still suggest everyone to watch it.
