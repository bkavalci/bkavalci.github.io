Maybe not a known band for many people but sometimes these bands perform beautiful musics and yes this is one of them. AMERICAN HORROR STORY

Horror movies? I always drop a question mark after them cos they don't do their main purpose, "terrify me". To be honest i can't say that "i was too scared" at this one as well, at least it didn't give me the feeling of Outlast or Amnesia and TWD when i was 12. But there is something else with this show, yes it is dark and yes it is not for everyone and because of that something else is different and that is plural kind of horror. American Horror Story offers us that. I haven't finish all the seasons yet but even right now i consider this show as good enough for "suggestable category", especially with the actoring skills of Evan Peters this show worth to watch.

LUCIFER

They don't make qualified tv shows anymore? Well they do and this is one of them. Our Fallen Angel of City Of Angels (yes i love that catchphrase) fighting against crime with LAPD. For Comic readers i've gotta say, storyline moving a bit seperated with it but it is still extremely dragging storyline. Unfortunately Lucifer, who had really successfull 3 seasons is about to remove from our screens. Even if they remove it and cut the show at half i still suggest everyone to watch it. This is one of those shows can actually make you laugh out of it purpose.

LOTR RETURN OF THE KING

I mean if someone still haven't watch this, i just want to ask "how cold was that cave you've been living?". Timeless classic should be enough to describe this but lets get in a bit more detail. Compare to other Hobbit and Lord of The Rings movies this one has better battle scenes... Well more likely i'm big fan of Viggo Mortensen (Aragorn) and i'm so happy to say that i had a chance to meet him IRL. Let's talk a bit more about the movie, if you ask me effects in the movie is brilliant and lets not forget LOTR series from the beginning of 2000's. About the storline, J.R.R Tolkien created better universe than ours i guess. Movie full with the actors i enjoy to watch for example Elijah Wood, Viggo Mortensen, Ian McKellen, Orlando Bloom, Christopher Lee. Those actors were on the movies like Green Street Holigans, The Road, Pirates of the Caribbean, X-Men and Star Wars. Well i guess we all know this is a great movie, if you were in a cave, right now get out and watch this movie.
